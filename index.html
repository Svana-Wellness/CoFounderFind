Our Vision

We’re creating a digital wellness sanctuary for modern seekers.

Guided by monks and rooted in India’s spiritual heritage — our goal is to make accessible, affordable wellness practices available to every 9–5 worker across the world.

We are building something far more scalable and relevant—a daily spiritual habit platform.

In a world where anxiety, burnout, and emotional fatigue are becoming the norm, we are introducing authentic, monk-led wellness practices that can be integrated seamlessly into everyone’s lives.

With a focus on authenticity, calming design, and culturally grounded experiences, Svana Wellness is here to bring sacred wisdom into modern life — quietly, consistently, and meaningfully.

Our Mission

  • Pre-recorded practices from monks and yogis in India’s spiritual hubs
  • Natural Indian soundscapes
  • Mindful storytelling and ancient bedtime tales
  • Guided breathwork and meditations rooted in yogic traditions

While global platforms like Calm and Headspace focus primarily on voice-led relaxation, we are offering the soul of India—immersive, visual, and spiritual. This is not just wellness. This is cultural, emotional, and mental realignment delivered with unmatched authenticity.

Svana Wellness – 5-Year Plan

Establish India’s First Digital Spiritual Wellness Brand: Launch a monk- and yogi-led platform offering immersive content rooted in Indian traditions — including meditations, breathwork, sleep stories, and sound healing — delivered through a premium mobile experience.

Build a Daily Wellness Habit in Millions of Lives: Become the go-to platform for 9-to-5 professionals seeking peace and clarity — helping users form consistent, life-changing spiritual habits, even with just 20 minutes a day.

Global Expansion with Cultural Authority: (targeting ₹100 Cr+ annual revenue by Year 5) Expand from India to major international wellness markets (U.S., U.K., Australia, UAE) by Year 2, bringing authentic Indian spiritual content to global users in localized formats and languages using AI.

Create the Largest Monk-Led Digital Library in the World: Collaborate with ashrams and spiritual teachers across India to build an ever-growing, trusted, high-quality library of wisdom — from yoga to dhyana to mantras to inner healing.

Position Svana as a Global Voice for Inner Transformation: Become a recognized leader in spiritual wellness, collaborating with global influencers, wellness retreats, and mindfulness platforms — transforming Svana from an app into a movement for mental and emotional evolution.
